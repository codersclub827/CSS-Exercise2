Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions

Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy, then remove and set aside. Butter one side of each slice of bread. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in the skillet. Layer with cheese, bacon, and tomato. Top with the second slice of bread, butter side up. Cook over medium heat until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, flipping once. Serve hot.
